The Problem

BFS, a wellness industry peer-networking and education provider, underwent a rebranding initiative. Their existing website ecosystem failed to reflect the new brand identity effectively, featuring outdated design and convoluted navigation.

2

The Solution

Collaborating closely with BFS, I modernized their website ecosystem using WordPress, Elementor, and custom HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. The redesign streamlined navigation, integrated cohesive branding elements, and delivered a visually stunning, user-friendly experience.

Weather Search Engine

Website

1

The Problem

Accessing accurate weather forecasts often involves navigating cumbersome interfaces or relying on multiple sources. Users face challenges in finding a simple, reliable solution for obtaining weather information for their desired locations.

2

The Solution

In response, I developed a streamlined weather search engine utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, integrated with a weather API. This solution offers users a user-friendly interface for accessing precise weather forecasts with ease.
